**Privacy Policy**

Last Updated: 2026-05-01.

StraGla is designed to run locally on your device for RTSP client streaming.

**Data Collection**

StraGla does not intentionally collect personal data on remote servers.

App settings are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to send logs manually, shared logs may include technical diagnostics such as device model, Android version, app version, and stream configuration details.

**Third-Party Services**

This build is intended to run without Play Store services, ads, and analytics integrations.

**Network Use**

When you configure an RTSP endpoint, network traffic is sent to that endpoint as part of normal streaming operation.

**Security**

No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure. Use secure endpoints and trusted networks where possible.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

This policy may be updated over time. Continued use of StraGla means you accept the latest version.
